— About the Doctor

A legacy of
healing in Raipur.

Dr. Satish Rathi is one of Chhattisgarh's most respected ENT specialists and cancer surgeons, practising for over three and a half decades at Rathi Clinic, Kalibadi Road, Raipur.

He completed his MBBS from Pt. Jawahar Lal Nehru Memorial Medical College, Raipur in 1988, and his postgraduate MS – ENT from Gandhi Medical College, Bhopal in 1993. He has practised at leading hospitals including Bombay Hospital, Nanavati Hospital, and Forte Hospital.

A registered member of the Indian Medical Association and AOI, Dr. Rathi has published research papers, participated in national ENT workshops, and received awards in otolaryngology. He is known for meticulous surgical technique and compassionate patient care.

— Specialisations

Treatments &
Procedures

Advanced ENT and head-neck surgical care under one expert roof.

Ear Micro Surgery
Precision microsurgical procedures for chronic ear disease, perforated eardrums, ossicular reconstruction, and hearing restoration.
FESS – Sinus Surgery
Functional Endoscopic Sinus Surgery for chronic sinusitis, nasal polyps, and sinus blockages using minimally invasive techniques.
Thyroid Surgery
Safe and precise surgical management of thyroid nodules, goitre, and thyroid cancers with focus on nerve preservation.
Head & Neck Cancer
Surgical treatment of cancers involving the larynx, pharynx, oral cavity, salivary glands, and neck lymph nodes.
Rhinoplasty
Surgical correction and cosmetic refinement of the nose for breathing improvement and aesthetic enhancement.
Foreign Body Removal
Safe removal of foreign objects from ears, nose, throat — especially critical in paediatric emergency cases.
Nasal Disorders
Treatment of deviated septum (DNS), allergic rhinitis, turbinate hypertrophy, and chronic nasal obstruction.
Ear Wax Removal
Professional cerumen removal using microsuction and irrigation to restore hearing and relieve discomfort.
Head & Neck Infections
Medical and surgical management of deep neck space infections, peritonsillar abscess, and related conditions.
